Service approach

Clean routines first. Better reporting follows.

Good bookkeeping depends on repeatable habits: matching source activity, resolving exceptions, reviewing balances, and making sure owners receive reports they can actually use.

Common monthly rhythm

Collect bank, card, bill, invoice, and source document activity.
Code transactions and follow up on unclear items.
Reconcile accounts and review balances.
Prepare owner-level reports and notes.
