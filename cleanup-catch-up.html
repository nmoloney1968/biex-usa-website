Common cleanup needs

When cleanup support helps.

Cleanup is useful when bank accounts have not been reconciled, credit cards are behind, old transactions are uncategorized, reports do not look right, or tax-time preparation keeps uncovering avoidable issues.

Scope can include

  • Prior-month transaction review
  • Bank and credit card reconciliation catch-up
  • Balance sheet review
  • A/R and A/P cleanup
  • Fixed asset review and depreciation support
  • Recommendations for a cleaner monthly process

Pricing note

Cleanup and catch-up work is quoted separately because backlog size, account count, software condition, and document availability can vary widely.
