Logo guidance:
- A logo is not strictly required to deploy the site.
- I included a simple starter logo and favicon so the site feels polished immediately.
- You can replace assets/logo.svg and assets/favicon.svg later without changing the page structure.

Deployment:
- Upload all files, preserving the assets folder.
- Point your hosting so index.html is the site root.
- If your host supports a custom 404 page, use 404.html.

AIPB badge support:
