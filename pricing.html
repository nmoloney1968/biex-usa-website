How is monthly pricing determined?

Pricing is influenced by transaction volume, the number of accounts, quality of source records, payroll complexity, and whether accounts payable or receivable support is needed.

Is cleanup work billed differently?

Yes. Historical cleanup, catch-up bookkeeping, and reconstruction work are typically scoped separately before moving into a recurring monthly package.

Can pricing change later?

If complexity increases materially, pricing may be adjusted at renewal or after a scope discussion. The goal is always to keep the arrangement clear and fair.
