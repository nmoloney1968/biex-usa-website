FAQ

Common questions

Do you replace my CPA or tax preparer?

No. We keep the books organized and current, then coordinate cleanly with your CPA or tax preparer when needed.

What software do you support?

We primarily support QuickBooks Online and Xero, plus practical digital workflows for receipts, source documents, and month-end reporting.

Can you help if my books are behind?

Yes. Cleanup and catch-up work is one of our core services. We review the state of the books, scope the issue, and propose a practical path back to current.
